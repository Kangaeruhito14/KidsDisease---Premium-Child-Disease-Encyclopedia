{{ t_dyn('About PediaCare') }}

PediaCare is a premium, open-access child disease encyclopedia designed to empower parents, caregivers, and pediatric students with structured clinical resource guides.

Clinical Methodology: Our data repository covers pediatric illnesses compiled from authoritative clinical references, international guidelines, and generic care manuals. Every profile is meticulously broken down into stages (Early, Progressive, Severe) to help track symptomatology transitions.

Information Encryption & Safety: Unlike standard static web resources, our complete clinical database is loaded in-memory through advanced client-side decrypters, protecting our content against automated web-scraping bots while maintaining lightning-fast performance.

{{ t_dyn('Privacy Policy') }}

{{ t_dyn('Effective Date: June 2026') }}

PediaCare is committed to protecting the privacy of its visitors. This Privacy Policy details our operational data patterns.

1. Complete Absence of Data Collection

We do not collect, request, or store any personally identifiable information (PII) or protected health information (PHI). Your clinical searches, autocomplete requests, triage checker entries, and vaccine schedule inputs are handled entirely inside your web browser in-memory and are never uploaded to any remote server.

2. Local Browser Storage

We utilize standard browser LocalStorage key/value pairs solely to store your language preferences (pediacare_lang) and dark theme state (pediacare_dark_mode) to ensure consistent page layouts across user sessions. No tracking cookies are deployed.

3. Multilingual Translations

Our multilingual features are powered by standard Google Translate integrations. Please review Google's privacy settings to understand how they process general translations.

4. Security Hardening

Strict Content Security Policies (CSP) are active. We enforce header framing protections to prevent clickjacking and session spoofing.
